1. What Are Cookies?  
Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, recognize you on return visits, and analyze site usage.

2. Types of Cookies We Use  
• Strictly Necessary: Required for the basic operation of our site and WhatsApp-based check-in.  
• Performance & Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site.  
• Functionality: Remember language or display preferences.  
• Targeting / Advertising: (None at this time.)

3. Legal Basis (GDPR)  
We set “Strictly Necessary” cookies without consent. All other cookies are set only after you click “Accept” on our cookie banner, based on your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

4. Managing Your Cookies  
• **Banner Controls**: Use the “Accept” button in our banner to enable non-essential cookies, or simply close the banner to decline.  
• **Browser Settings**: You can delete or block cookies via your browser settings. See www.allaboutcookies.org for guides.  
• **Opt-Out**: To opt-out of Google Analytics, install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

5. Third-Party Cookies  
We embed scripts (e.g. Google Analytics, WhatsApp Business) that may set cookies. We do not share any PII with these services—they only receive anonymized usage data (e.g. “page visited”).
